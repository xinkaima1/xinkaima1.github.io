## CRAIC Simuro Football Project

The **China Robot Artificial Intelligence Competition (CRAIC)** Simuro Football project is a simulated robot soccer competition where teams develop AI strategies to control virtual robots in a football match.

### Project Overview

This project focuses on developing intelligent soccer strategies for multi-robot coordination:
- ⚽ **Multi-Agent Coordination** - Teamwork and role assignment among robots
- 🧠 **Game Strategy** - Offensive and defensive tactics adaptation
- 🎯 **Motion Planning** - Precise movement and ball control algorithms
- 🏆 **Competitive AI** - Real-time decision making against opponents

### Technologies

- **Simuro Simulation Platform** - Robot soccer simulation environment
- **Strategy Algorithms** - Finite state machines and behavior trees
- **Path Planning** - Collision-free trajectory generation
- **Game Theory** - Optimal decision making in competitive scenarios

### Role & Contributions

As a team member at Jilin University, I contribute to:
- Strategy design and algorithm implementation
- Robot movement and ball control optimization
- Match analysis and strategy improvement
