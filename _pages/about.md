## About Me

Hello! I'm **Xinkai Ma (马鑫凯)**, an undergraduate student at **Jilin University**.

I'm currently studying in the **College of Biological and Agricultural Engineering** (2023-2026), with a background in Life Sciences (2022-2023). My academic journey has given me a unique interdisciplinary perspective, bridging biology with engineering principles.

## Research & Projects

I'm passionate about robotics and AI competitions. I'm actively involved in the **CRAIC (China Robot Artificial Intelligence Competition)** projects:

- **🌙 Lunar Exploration Project** - Developing autonomous navigation and exploration systems for lunar rover robots
- **⚽ Simuro Football Project** - Building AI strategies for simulated robot soccer competitions

These projects allow me to apply engineering knowledge to solve real-world robotic challenges, from path planning to decision-making algorithms.
